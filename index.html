Chicamocha Canyon

A steep sided canyon carved by the Chicamocha River in Colombia. With a maximum depth of 2,000 metres, an area of 108,000 hectares and a length of 227 kilometres, the canyon is the second-largest worldwide.

Barichara

Barichara is a town in northern Colombia known for its cobbled streets and colonial architecture. In the center, the sandstone Catedral de la Inmaculada Concepción features a gold-leaf altar.
